360 Degree Pizzeria is a family business located in Nairobi, Kenya. We offer traditional Neapolitan style pizza made from nothing with fresh and locally sourced ingredients. Our recipes have been tried and tested for years to be able to provide you with the best version of pizza. All our pizzas are cooked in our custom made wood fired oven which was designed and manufactured in Italy. Feel free to visit us at our restaurant at Sarit Centre, Karuna Rd, Nairobi to have a taste of our incredibe pizzas!. And we also do deliveries so you can enjoy our pizzas in the comfort of your home, just make your order down below.
